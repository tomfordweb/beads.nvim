# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want beads.nvim to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in discussions, issues, and pull requests.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Assume good faith and focus on what's best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **tomfordweb@gmail.com**.
All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially. The maintainer may
take any action deemed appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or
permanent removal from the project's spaces.
